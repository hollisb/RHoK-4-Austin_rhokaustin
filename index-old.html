Home

Random Hacks of Kindness is a global community of innovators building practical open technology to make the world a better place. We want developers, designers, and engineers to use technology to help solve social and environmental problems all over the world. RHoK.org sponsors hackathons year round, and all over the world; but the main event is a semi-annual worldwide hackathon. This winter is the first time Austin, TX will be a host city.

Come join us in Austin TX for this "Hackathon for Social Good" December 2nd through December 4th. You can sign up at the Eventbrite page.

FAQ

  • Who should come?
    We need all sorts of people to make RHoK Austin successful. We need code monkeys, css ninjas, HTML pirates, project management gurus, design afficianados...You get the picture. If you like building software, have a passion for social change, or just like hanging around people who do, then this is the place for you.
  • Do I have to stay the whole time?
    No. Come for just the reception, or just Saturday, or the whole weekend. We don't care. Give us your time and energy, and it'll be greatly rewarded with caffeine and prizes.
  • What do I get if I participate?
    Much like a marathon we'll provide you with a free t-shirt and some food. Unlike a marathon you'll also get an overwhelming sense of having done something meaningful with your weekend (sorry runners, but you know it's true.)
  • How much does it cost?
    Nothing. Nada. Zilch. Our generous sponsors have covered all of the costs (including feeding your caffeine addiction,) so that you can bring the magic.
  • What will we work on?
    What ever you want, so long as it's for social good. RHoK has a number of problems available to choose from, or you can bring your own and excite a team of folks into helping you make it a reality. We'll have a few Subject Matter Experts on hand to represent and champion a few different ideas.
